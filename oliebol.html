Oliebol

Information

This dish evolved over the years and could be seen as Dutch doughnuts. The first people who made a version of the oliebol where called the Batavi and Frisii, both part of the Germanic tribe. Back then, the dish was called oliekoek, a flat version of the oliebol. The earliest discovered recipe of oliekoeken was found in a Dutch cookbook published in 1667. Around the same time, oil became more abundantly available and people could use it more. This made it possible for the flat version to become sphere- shaped. The oliebol was born.
