Poffertjes

Ingredients

  • 60 gram butter
  • 120 gram sugar
  • 175 gram self-rising flour
  • Butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 decilitres milk
  • Salt
Product Quantity
Butter 60 gram
Sugar 120 gram
Self-rising flour 175 gram
Eggs 2
Milk 2 decilitres
Salt

Preperation

First, mix the butter and sugar together until a batter. Add the eggs and mix the milk, salt and flour trough the batter. After this, you can grease the 'poffertjespan' with butter. The last step is to fill the 'poffertjespan' with the dough. When the poffertjes are light brown, they are ready.
