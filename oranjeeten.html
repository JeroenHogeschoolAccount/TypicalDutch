Hutspot

Information

This national dish of mashed potatoes and veggies is mostly eaten in the winter months. However, for a long time, this dish was most populair during the harvest months because the nutritional qualities of the potatoes mixed with carrots and onions gave farmers enough energy to get through the long and hard days. The term hutspot dates from the 16th century and is generally an one-pot dish that is easily and delicious.
