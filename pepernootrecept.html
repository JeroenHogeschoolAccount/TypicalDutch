Pepernoten

Ingredients

  • 140 gram unsalted butter
  • 120 gram sugar
  • 2 tbsp speculaas spices
  • 240 gram self-rising flour
  • 3.5 tbsp milk
  • 0.5 tsp salt
Product Quantity
Unsalted butter 140 gram
Sugar 120 gram
Speculaas spices 2 tbsp
Self-rising flour 240 gram
Salt 0,5 tsp
Self-rising flour 240 gram

Preperation

First, heat the oven to 150/160 C. Add all ingredients together and knead it. After this, make 4 long rolls of the dough (1cm). Cut the roll into pieces and make balls of it. Put the balls on the baking tray - which is covered with baking paper. Leave it for about 15 minutes in the oven until the pepernoten are light brown.
