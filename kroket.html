Kroket

Information

This snack is similar to the bitterbal, only the shape is different. Instead of being shaped like a ball, the kroket is sausage-shaped. There are different varieties of the kroket in the Netherlands: rundvleeskroket (made with beef), kalfsvleeskroket (made with veal) and the satekroket (made with satay sauce and meat). MacDonalds even sells their version on a bun as McKroket.

Souce: Students HvA, 2019. International Student Guide Amsterdam
